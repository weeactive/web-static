Japan Tourist Spots

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is the highest mountain in Japan, standing at 3,776 meters (12,388 feet). It is an active volcano that last erupted in 1707-1708. The mountain is considered sacred by many Japanese people and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park

The Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park is a park in the center of Hiroshima, Japan, dedicated to the legacy of Hiroshima as the first city in the world to suffer a nuclear attack. The park contains a museum and several memorials, including the A-Bomb Dome, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

The Arashiyama Bamboo Grove is a bamboo forest in the Arashiyama district of Kyoto, Japan. The grove is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Kyoto and is especially beautiful in the early morning or late afternoon, when the light is filtered through the bamboo.
